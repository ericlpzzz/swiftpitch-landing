FAQs

What is SwiftPitch?

SwiftPitch is an AI-powered tool that creates freelance proposals based on job listings from platforms like Upwork and Freelancer.com. Just paste the job link, upload your resume or LinkedIn, and get a tailored proposal in seconds.

How is this different from templates or ChatGPT prompts?

Templates are generic. SwiftPitch reads the actual job post and personalizes the proposal based on your profile, tone, and niche—so it feels custom-written without taking hours.

Will this actually help me win more jobs?

While no tool can guarantee wins, SwiftPitch is designed to improve response rates by sending more relevant, well-structured proposals faster—freeing you to focus on high-quality gigs.

Which platforms are supported?

Currently, SwiftPitch supports Upwork and Freelancer.com. Support for Fiverr, LinkedIn Jobs, and AngelList is in development.

Do I need to create an account to try it?

No account needed for the Free Plan. You can try it instantly. Upgrade plans let you save your history and get more customization.

What happens when I run out of proposals in my plan?

You can either wait for your monthly quota to reset, or purchase extra proposals with our pay-as-you-go option.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We do not share or sell your data. Resumes and proposals are processed securely, and stored only if you choose to save them in your account.

Can I use this for team or agency accounts?

Yes. Our Agency Plan supports multiple profiles and white-label proposals. Perfect for freelancers working at scale or small agencies managing multiple clients.
